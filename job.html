Project Architect

Metropolis, NY • Full-time

About The Role

AGAP is seeking a talented and motivated Project Architect with 5-7 years of professional experience to join our collaborative studio. In this role, you will be integral to the design and delivery of high-profile projects from conception through construction administration. You will work closely with firm principals, project teams, and clients to develop innovative design solutions and ensure the highest level of quality in all project phases.

What You'll Do

  • Lead the development of design concepts and technical documentation for a range of project types.
  • Manage project timelines, budgets, and consultant coordination to ensure project goals are met.
  • Mentor junior staff and contribute to a collaborative studio environment.
  • Prepare and deliver client presentations, effectively communicating design intent.
  • Oversee the construction administration phase, including site visits, submittal reviews, and RFI responses.

What You'll Bring

  • A professional degree in Architecture (B.Arch or M.Arch).
  • Architectural licensure is required.
  • 5-7 years of post-graduate, professional experience, preferably in high-end residential or hospitality projects.
  • Proficiency in Revit, AutoCAD, Rhino, and Adobe Creative Suite.
  • Strong design sensibility and a portfolio of built work that demonstrates technical excellence.
  • Excellent communication and leadership skills.
