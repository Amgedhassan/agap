The Minimalist Retreat

Client

Eleanor Vance

Location

Aspen, Colorado

Category

Residential

Year

2023

A Dialogue Between Nature and Structure

Tasked with designing a secluded sanctuary in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, our vision was to create a structure that listens to the landscape. The client, a novelist seeking solitude and inspiration, desired a space that was both grand in its views and intimate in its living experience.

We employed a palette of locally sourced limestone, blackened steel, and vast panes of glass to blur the lines between inside and out. The architectural form is a series of clean, intersecting lines that frame the dramatic mountain vistas, ensuring every room offers a unique connection to the surrounding nature. The interior design follows a minimalist philosophy, prioritizing texture and light over ornamentation, allowing the client's life and the changing seasons to be the primary focus.
